## Minor Arcana – Suit Flavor Hooks

- **Tokens (Earth)** – “Value fortification, emerald glows around vaults, +1 defense, commander whales issuing orders.”
- **Liquidity (Water)** – “Aqua conduits weaving pools, healing ripples, blue/purple neon, LP avatars celebrating flow.”
- **Security (Air)** – “Crimson shields, audit sigils, undestroyable walls, eagle-eyed sentinels intercepting hacks.”
- **Nodes (Fire)** – “Neon reactors, blazing validator towers, rapid marches, founders coding mid-battle.”

### Court Archetypes

- **Page** – “Messenger spark delivering fresh intel or heals.”
- **Knight** – “Charging trooper amplifying attack or defense.”
- **Queen** – “Aura caster buffing entire formation.”
- **King** – “Strategist issuing suit-wide command; ledger or console in hand.”
