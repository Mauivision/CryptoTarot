## 🎲 Rarity Effects

### Genesis (NFT Rarity)

- **Effect:** Double all card effects
- **Example:** +2 chain becomes +4 chain

### Legendary (NFT Rarity)

- **Effect:** +50% to all effects
- **Example:** +2 chain becomes +3 chain

### Epic (NFT Rarity)

- **Effect:** +25% to all effects
- **Example:** +2 chain becomes +2.5 chain (rounds to 3)

### Rare (NFT Rarity)

- **Effect:** +10% to all effects
- **Example:** +2 chain becomes +2.2 chain (rounds to 2)

### Common (Standard)

- **Effect:** Base effects only
- **No modifications**

## 📊 Turn Structure

1. **Draw Phase** - Draw 1 card
2. **Play Phase** - Play 1 card (Major or Minor)
3. **Resolve Phase** - Apply effects
4. **End Phase** - Check victory conditions

### Victory Conditions

- **Chain Victory:** Reach 21 chain score
- **Rally Victory:** Control 5+ units
- **Battle Victory:** Opponent has 0 units
- **Time Victory:** Most chain score after 10 turns

---

## 🎯 Strategy Tips

### Early Game

- Focus on building chain score
- Play Minor Arcana for consistent value
- Save Major Arcana for key moments

### Mid Game

- Use suit bonuses strategically
- Combine cards for combo effects
- Control the board with units

### Late Game

- Use Major Arcana for game-changing effects
- Protect your chain score
- Disrupt opponent's strategy
