# Crypto Tarot: Chain Game Reference Sheet

Pocket guide for live play. Pairs with `chain-game/README.md`.

---

## Turn Structure

- **Draw** 1 card.
- **Rally** minors to your back row (pay value in energy).
- **March** forward (pay value again; max 3 rows per turn).
- **Battle** opposing front rows; power compares, suits apply bonuses.
- **Spell** 1 Major Arcana per turn (discard after effect).
- **Win** by reaching 21 blocks or reducing opponent Life to 0.

## Minor Arcana – Suit Summary

### Tokens (Earth – Value)

- **Suit bonus:** +1 value to the chain, +1 defense in battle.
- **Courts:** Page draw 1; Knight +1 power attacking; Queen steal 1 Life on win; King army +1 power.

### Liquidity (Water – Healing)

- **Suit bonus:** Heal 1 Life on rally; +1 energy every 3-card chain; +1 power vs. Security.
- **Courts:** Page heal 2; Knight +2 power defending; Queen team heal 1 on win; King swap 1 card with opponent.

### Security (Air – Defense)

- **Suit bonus:** Blocks can’t be destroyed; +2 defense; +1 power vs. Nodes.
- **Courts:** Page +2 defense; Knight cannot be stolen; Queen allies +1 defense; King reveal opponent hand.

### Nodes (Fire – Speed)

- **Suit bonus:** +1 march; +1 power per Nodes in chain; +1 power vs. Tokens.
- **Courts:** Page draw 1 tech card; Knight +2 march; Queen all allies +1 march; King doubles next effect.

**Numbered cards (Ace–10):** value = face number, baseline power = same. Suit bonuses apply automatically.

## Quick Reminders

- **Energy =** sum of values on your back row.
- **March cost =** card value.
- **Rally =** place card from hand to back row (pay value).
- **Battle =** compare power + suit bonuses; losing block destroyed.
- **Surrender** a battle to retreat 1 row (no damage).
- **Companion App:** log blocks/Life, apply NFT checks automatically.

Play boldly. The chain is watching.
