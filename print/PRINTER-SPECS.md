## 🎴 Card Specifications

### Physical Card Dimensions

- **Size:** Standard Tarot (2.75" × 4.75" / 70mm × 120mm)
- **Quantity:** 78 cards per deck
- **Card Stock:** 350gsm premium cardstock
- **Finish:** Matte lamination with rounded corners
- **Packaging:** Custom tuck box with foil stamping

### Design Requirements

- **Bleed:** 0.125" (3mm) on all sides
- **Safe Zone:** 0.25" (6mm) from edges
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI minimum
- **Color Mode:** CMYK for print, RGB for digital preview
- **File Format:** PDF/X-1a or PDF/X-4
