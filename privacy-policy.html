Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

1. No Data Collection

Crypto Tarot does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you. We do not use analytics tracking, cookies for tracking purposes, or any third-party data collection services.

2. Local Storage Only

The only data stored is in your browser's local storage, which includes:

  • OpenAI API Key (optional): If you choose to use AI-powered readings, your API key is stored locally in your browser only. We never see or transmit this key.
  • Disclaimer Dismissal: A simple flag indicating you've dismissed the disclaimer banner, so it doesn't show again.

All local storage data remains on your device and is never sent to our servers (we don't have any servers).

3. No Tracking

We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or any analytics tools
  • Advertising trackers
  • Social media pixels
  • Third-party cookies
  • Fingerprinting technologies

4. External Services

The only external services we use are:

  • Google Fonts: For typography. Your browser may connect to Google's servers to load fonts, which is subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • OpenAI API (optional): Only if you choose to use AI-powered readings and provide your own API key. Your API key and requests go directly to OpenAI, not through our servers.

5. GDPR Compliance

Since we don't collect any personal data, GDPR requirements for data processing don't apply. However, we respect your privacy rights:

  • You have the right to clear your local storage at any time
  • No personal data is collected, so there's nothing to request, modify, or delete
  • You can use the site without providing any information

6. Your Control

You have complete control:

  • Clear your browser's local storage to remove any stored data
  • Use the site without enabling any optional features
  • Block third-party requests if desired (though this may affect font loading)

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Our Commitment: Your privacy is important to us. Crypto Tarot is designed to be private by default—we simply don't collect data because we don't need it.
